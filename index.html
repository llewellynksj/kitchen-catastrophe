Kitchen Catastrophe!

Help! We need you!

Can you help our kitchen team unscramble a whole bunch of food and drink words so they can create their menu?

Here's what you need to know:

  • Answer 3 in a row correctly and move up to the next level
  • Complete all 4 levels and you win!
  • Answer 3 in a row incorrectly and you'll have to start again
  • Level 1: Start off easy with 3 and 4 letter words
  • Level 2: A bit more tricky; 5 and 6 letter words
  • Level 3: Uh-oh, 7 and 8 letter words! We've also thrown in some cooking techniques to really test you
  • The Final Level: Can you solve the 10 letter words?!
Start unscrambling!
