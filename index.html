How to Play Play the Game

Kitchen Catastrophe!

Help! We need you!

Can you help our chefs unscramble these food and drink words so they can create their menu?

Here's what you need to know:

  • Answer 2 correctly and move to the next level
  • Answer 3 incorrectly and you'll have to start again
  • Complete all 6 levels and you win!
  • Level 1:4 and 5 letter FOOD words
  • Level 2:4 and 5 letter DRINK words
  • Level 3:6 to 8 letter FOOD words
  • Level 4:6 to 8 letter DRINK words
  • Level 5:4 to 8 letter COOKING METHOD words
  • Final Level:10 letter words; food, drink & cooking methods!
Start unscrambling!
